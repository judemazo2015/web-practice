Steps for Enrollment

  1. Fill out forms. This involves completing an enrollment or application form, which may be done online or in person. Submit requirements.
  2. The school's admissions office will evaluate your submitted forms and documents.
  3. You'll proceed to the appropriate dean's or department office to select your subjects or courses for the semester.
  4. The registrar's office will provide you with a detailed assessment of the required tuition and other fees.
  5. After payment, you can claim your printed registration form from the registrar's office.
  6. You are now officially enrolled, which might be confirmed by a digital certificate on your student portal or email.
